# 🔥 Dragon Poker
### *A game of wits, weyrs, and draconic hierarchy*

> **2–6 players · 69-card deck · 5 suits**

---

## At a Glance

| Cards per Hand | Hatching Ground | Turns per Round | Wildcards |
|:-:|:-:|:-:|:-:|
| 7 | 3 | 5 | 4 |

## The Five Suits

Dragon color **always** overrides card number. A 3 of Bronzes beats a 10 of Browns.

| Rank | Suit | Dragons |
|------|------|---------|
| Highest | **Golds** | Queen dragons |
| High | **Bronzes** | Bronze dragons |
| Mid | **Browns** | Brown dragons |
| Low | **Blues** | Blue dragons |
| Lowest | **Greens** | Green dragons |

## Setup

- **Deal** — Each player is dealt seven cards.
- **Hatching Ground** — Three cards are set face-up on the table as communal cards available to all players.
- **Clutch Card** — One card is placed face-down below the Hatching Ground, revealed at endgame.

---

## Turn Structure

```
1. Weyr Bell        → Open betting
2. Pass / Call      → or Impress
3. The Reveal       → Flip all cards
4. Draw or swap     → Hatching Ground
```

---

## Special Cards

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 👑 **Weyrwoman** | Declare as any Gold value of your choice. |
| ⚔️ **Weyrleader** | Declare as any Bronze value of your choice. |
| 🔄 **Weyrsecond** | Force one opponent to swap a card with the Hatching Ground. |
| 🥚 **Impression** *(×4)* | Wild — any card, any suit. |

---

## The Clutch Card

One card is placed face-down beneath the Hatching Ground and revealed after 5 turns.

| Result | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Suit match** | Matches winner's suit — double the pot. |
| **Value match** | Matches any card in winning hand — second place may challenge with a draw. |
| **Impression card** | Entire pot rolls to next game — a **"Gold Flight"** pot. |

---

## Weyr Etiquette

- 👁️ A rider may not fold while their dragon is physically present and watching. The dragon's pride is at stake.
- 🍷 If a green rider wins a Gold Flight pot, tradition demands they buy Benden wine for the whole table.
- ⚖️ All disputes are settled by the senior Weyrwoman, whose ruling is final and binding.
